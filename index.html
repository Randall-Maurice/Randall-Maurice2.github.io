Welcome Reader, to my Home Page

Hello and welcome to my webpage, A Scholar's Life in Quarantine.

This is a Comsci Project applying our basic knowledge whilst making it informative and tackle a current socioeconomic problem. I have picked how quarantine affects my fellow scholars and I, and it contains our different insights, their actual responses and many more relating to those stuff. Just a simple click will, hopefully, present you the proper content you are willing to see.

It may be simple so please bare with me. On the right side, there are choices for you to click and navigate around which are,
1 a student, containing my own opinions, ideas and other insights from the current school set-up which is online learning.
2 a group of hands risen, emphasizing on the group, this is where you will locate a few of my fellow batchmates explaining their insights and sharing their own opinions, and their responses are varied yet united in some way.
3 a light bulb, which it may represent as "Idea". Here I tried to compile what my fellow scholars and I thought of as Pro's and Con's of online learning and try to compare it back when we were attending Face to Face classes.
